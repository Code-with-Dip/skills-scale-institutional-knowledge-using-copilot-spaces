# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove blockers, help the team adopt practices, and collaborate with both PM and Developers to maintain momentum and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, sprint reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove impediments and shield the team from disruptions
- Support continuous improvement practices and process optimization
- Coach the team on agile methodologies and best practices
- Track team velocity and sprint metrics

### Goals
- Enable the team to deliver at a sustainable pace
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and psychological safety
- Reduce friction and maximize team productivity

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and ceremony facilitation
- One-on-ones with team members
- Coaching and mentoring discussions

### Interactions
- Works closely with Project Manager, Developers, and Product Manager to maintain team health and delivery momentum

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure the product's usability, accessibility, and positive user experience. They translate requirements into wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs informed by user research.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Conduct user research, usability testing, and accessibility audits
- Advocate for user needs in prioritization and design reviews
- Collaborate on design systems and consistency standards
- Validate designs with users before implementation

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support adoption
- Ensure designs meet both user and business needs

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and insights
- Design specs and component documentation

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Manager, Developers, QA, and Customer Support to ensure user-centered solutions

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide insights through data collection, reporting, and analysis to inform product decisions and measure success metrics. They translate data into actionable intelligence.

### Responsibilities
- Design tracking plans and instrumentation strategies
- Build and maintain dashboards for key product metrics
- Analyze user behavior and product performance data
- Inform decision-making with evidence and statistical insights
- Monitor success metrics defined in project charters

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across the organization
- Identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for optimization
- Measure and demonstrate the impact of delivered features

### Typical Communication
- Metric definitions and tracking plan documentation
- Dashboards and regular data reports
- Analysis findings and recommendations

### Interactions
- Works with Product Manager, Developers, and Stakeholders to define, track, and interpret success metrics

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads advise and support the team in identifying threats, implementing controls, and adhering to security best practices throughout the delivery lifecycle. They are partners in building secure-by-design solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct threat modeling and security design reviews
- Guide implementation of secure development practices and controls
- Perform security testing and code reviews
- Triage security incidents and coordinate response
- Ensure compliance with organizational policies and standards
- Advocate for security in prioritization and trade-off decisions

### Goals
- Minimize security risks and vulnerabilities in delivered features
- Foster a security-aware culture within the team
- Ensure compliance with applicable regulations and standards

### Typical Communication
- Security design reviews and threat assessments
- Security testing reports and remediation guidance
- Incident triage and response coordination

### Interactions
- Partners with Developers, Product Manager, and Project Manager to integrate security throughout delivery

---

## Customer Support Liaison

### Role Summary
Customer Support Liaisons represent the support or customer-facing teams, ensuring user needs and pain points are communicated to the delivery team and properly prioritized. They are the voice of the customer in product decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Share customer feedback, common pain points, and support trends
- Act as the voice of customer in planning, reviews, and prioritization
- Help validate solutions against real customer needs
- Close the loop with customer communications after releases
- Identify patterns in support tickets and escalations
- Advocate for usability and support considerations in design

### Goals
- Reduce support burden and customer friction
- Ensure delivered features address real customer pain points
- Improve customer satisfaction and retention

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and trend reports
- Planning participation and feedback sessions
- Post-release announcements and customer communications

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Manager, Developers, UX Designer, and Stakeholders to ensure customer-centric solutions

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- When a persona is not explicitly assigned to a project, adapt these role descriptions to your team's structure.
